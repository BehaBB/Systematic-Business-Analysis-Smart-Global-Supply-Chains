# Business Context Analysis
## Baikal Fish Processing Plant - Digital Transformation

### Company Profile
- **Name**: Baikal Fish Processing Plant
- **Location**: Irkutsk Region, Lake Baikal shoreline
- **Founded**: 1998
- **Annual Revenue**: $3.2 million

### Core Business Activities
1. **Fish Harvesting**: Omul, whitefish, grayling
2. **Processing**:
   - Smoking (cold/hot)
   - Freezing
   - Drying
   - Canning
3. **Distribution**: Russia through regional distributors

### Current Challenges
#### 1. Production Planning
- Manual scheduling using Excel spreadsheets
- No integration with catch data
- Seasonal fluctuations not systematically accounted for

#### 2. Quality Management
- Paper-based quality control logs
- Manual entry of laboratory results
- No end-to-end batch tracking

#### 3. Inventory Management
- Discrepancies between physical and book quantities
- Processing losses not analyzed
- Manual stock counting and reconciliation

### Digital Transformation Objectives
1. **Production Optimization** (+8% product yield)
2. **Loss Reduction** (28% → 12%)
3. **Reporting Automation** (16hr → 4hr per week)
4. **145% ROI** within 18 months

### Critical Processes for Automation
- Production capacity planning
- Raw material and finished goods quality control
- Raw material tracking and yield calculation
- Production reporting and analytics
