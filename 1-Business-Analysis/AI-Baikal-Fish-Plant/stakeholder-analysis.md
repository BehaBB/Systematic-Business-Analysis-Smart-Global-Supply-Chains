# Stakeholder Analysis
## Baikal Fish Processing Plant Digital Transformation

## Key Stakeholder Groups

### Executive Leadership
- **Plant Director**: Primary decision-maker, focused on ROI and operational efficiency
- **Finance Manager**: Concerned with cost reduction and financial metrics
- **Operations Director**: Interested in production optimization and quality improvement

### Operational Teams
- **Production Manager**: Oversees processing operations and scheduling
- **Quality Control Team**: Responsible for product quality and safety standards
- **Inventory Manager**: Manages raw material and finished goods inventory
- **Maintenance Supervisor**: Equipment reliability and maintenance scheduling

### Technical Teams
- **IT Coordinator**: System implementation and technical support
- **Data Analyst**: Performance monitoring and reporting
- **Lab Technicians**: Quality testing and compliance monitoring

### External Stakeholders
- **Fishermen Cooperatives**: Raw material suppliers
- **Distribution Partners**: Regional distributors and retailers
- **Regulatory Authorities**: Food safety and environmental compliance

## Change Management Approach

### High Influence/High Interest
- **Engagement**: Active participation in design and implementation
- **Communication**: Direct and frequent updates
- **Involvement**: Decision-making authority

### High Influence/Low Interest  
- **Engagement**: Regular briefings on critical decisions
- **Communication**: Concise, business-focused updates
- **Involvement**: Approval on key milestones

### Low Influence/High Interest
- **Engagement**: Training and support programs
- **Communication**: Detailed operational updates
- **Involvement**: Feedback collection and process input

### Low Influence/Low Interest
- **Engagement**: General awareness campaigns
- **Communication**: Broad organizational updates
- **Involvement**: Basic training and orientation

## Risk Mitigation by Stakeholder

### Resistant Stakeholders
- **Quality Control Team**: Demonstrate time savings and error reduction
- **Senior Operators**: Highlight ease of use and job enhancement

### Critical Dependencies
- **IT Infrastructure**: Ensure system compatibility and performance
- **Data Migration**: Plan for historical data preservation
- **Training Programs**: Address digital literacy gaps
