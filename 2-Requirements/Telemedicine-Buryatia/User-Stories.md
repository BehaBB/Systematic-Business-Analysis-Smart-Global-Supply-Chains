# User Stories - Buryatia Telemedicine Platform

## Epic 1: Patient Registration and Onboarding

### Story 1: Bilingual Registration
**As a** Buryat-speaking elderly patient  
**I want** to register in my native language  
**So that** I can understand the medical registration process completely

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Registration form available in both Buryat and Russian languages
- Voice interface supports Buryat pronunciation
- Cultural mediators available for assistance during registration
- Offline paper-based registration option for areas with no internet
- Simple step-by-step process with large, clear fonts

### Story 2: Cultural Consent Process
**As a** patient who values traditional healing  
**I want** to provide informed consent for traditional medicine integration  
**So that** my cultural beliefs are respected in treatment decisions

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Clear explanation of how traditional medicine will be integrated
- Option to choose preferred treatment approach (modern, traditional, or both)
- Traditional healer involvement requires explicit patient consent
- Cultural mediator available to explain consent terms
- Consent can be modified at any time

## Epic 2: Teleconsultation Management

### Story 3: Low-Bandwidth Video Consultation
**As a** patient in a remote area with poor internet  
**I want** to have video consultations that work with limited bandwidth  
**So that** I can access healthcare without traveling long distances

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Video calls work with minimum 64 kbps internet speed
- Automatic quality adjustment based on connection speed
- Audio-only fallback when video is not possible
- Connection stability indicators visible during calls
- Offline message capability when connection is lost

### Story 4: Traditional Medicine Integration
**As a** patient who trusts traditional healing methods  
**I want** to see compatible traditional treatment options  
**So that** I can make informed decisions about my healthcare

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- System suggests traditional alternatives alongside modern treatments
- Clear compatibility warnings for conflicting treatments
- Traditional healer recommendations integrated into treatment plan
- Cultural context provided for each traditional treatment
- Local availability of traditional remedies indicated

## Epic 3: Medical Data Management

### Story 5: Multilingual Health Records
**As a** healthcare provider working with diverse patients  
**I want** to access medical records in both Russian and Buryat  
**So that** I can provide culturally appropriate care

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Patient records display in chosen language preference
- Medical terminology properly translated between languages
- Traditional medicine treatments recorded alongside modern ones
- Cultural notes section for provider reference
- Privacy controls for sensitive cultural information

### Story 6: Traditional Knowledge Database
**As a** traditional healer  
**I want** to contribute to and access the traditional medicine database  
**So that** our ancestral knowledge is preserved and shared appropriately

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Secure login for verified traditional healers
- Intellectual property protection for contributed knowledge
- Cultural approval process for new entries
- Search functionality by symptoms and local plant names
- Usage tracking and benefit sharing mechanism

## Epic 4: Medication and Treatment Management

### Story 7: Bilingual Medication Instructions
**As a** patient with limited Russian proficiency  
**I want** to receive medication instructions in my native language  
**So that** I understand how to take my medicine correctly

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- All prescriptions include Buryat language instructions
- Voice instructions available for illiterate patients
- Visual aids for dosage and timing
- Traditional remedy preparation instructions included
- Drug interaction warnings in both languages

### Story 8: Integrated Treatment Plans
**As a** district doctor  
**I want** to create treatment plans that combine modern and traditional approaches  
**So that** I can provide comprehensive care that respects patient preferences

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Treatment plan templates for common conditions
- Compatibility checking between modern and traditional treatments
- Traditional healer collaboration tools
- Patient preference settings respected
- Progress tracking for combined treatments

## Epic 5: Community and Cultural Features

### Story 9: Cultural Mediator Dashboard
**As a** cultural mediator  
**I want** to monitor patient engagement and cultural acceptance  
**So that** I can identify and address cultural barriers to care

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Dashboard showing language preference usage
- Cultural acceptance metrics tracking
- Patient feedback collection tools
- Traditional healer engagement statistics
- Community outreach coordination features

### Story 10: Elderly Patient Support
**As a** elderly patient with limited technology experience  
**I want** simple, voice-guided navigation through the application  
**So that** I can use telemedicine services independently

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Voice command interface in Buryat language
- Large touch targets and simple menu structure
- Family member assistance mode available
- Step-by-step audio guidance for all features
- Emergency contact quick access

## Epic 6: System Administration

### Story 11: Multi-language Content Management
**As a** system administrator  
**I want** to manage content in multiple languages efficiently  
**So that** the platform remains culturally relevant and accurate

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Centralized content management system
- Translation workflow with cultural validation
- Version control for all language content
- Bulk update capabilities for common terms
- Cultural review and approval process

### Story 12: Connectivity Management
**As a** technical support specialist  
**I want** to monitor connectivity across different regions  
**So that** I can optimize performance for remote areas

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Real-time connectivity status dashboard
- Automatic fallback to lower bandwidth modes
- Offline functionality monitoring
- User-reported connectivity issues tracking
- Regional performance analytics

## Priority and Implementation Timeline

### High Priority (Phase 1 - Months 1-3)
- Stories 1, 3, 5, 7 (Core patient functionality)
- Basic bilingual interface and low-bandwidth video

### Medium Priority (Phase 2 - Months 4-6)  
- Stories 2, 4, 6, 8 (Traditional medicine integration)
- Cultural features and healer collaboration

### Lower Priority (Phase 3 - Months 7-9)
- Stories 9, 10, 11, 12 (Advanced features)
- Administration and optimization tools
