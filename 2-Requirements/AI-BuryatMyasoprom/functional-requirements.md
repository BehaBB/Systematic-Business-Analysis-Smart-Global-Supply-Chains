# Functional Requirements: AI BuryatMyasoprom

## 1. Customs Automation Module

### 1.1 Document Generation
- **FR-001**: System shall auto-generate 25+ required China customs documents
- **FR-002**: System shall populate documents from product database
- **FR-003**: System shall validate document completeness before submission
- **FR-004**: System shall track document status through approval process

### 1.2 Regulation Management
- **FR-005**: System shall maintain database of China import regulations
- **FR-006**: System shall alert users to regulation changes
- **FR-007**: System shall validate products against current restrictions

## 2. Quality Traceability Module

### 2.1 Supply Chain Tracking
- **FR-008**: System shall track meat batches from farm to Chinese retail
- **FR-009**: System shall record quality inspections at each transfer point
- **FR-010**: System shall generate traceability reports for Chinese partners

### 2.2 Quality Monitoring
- **FR-011**: System shall integrate with IoT temperature sensors
- **FR-012**: System shall trigger alerts for temperature deviations
- **FR-013**: System shall predict shelf life based on storage conditions

## 3. Compliance Management

### 3.1 Standards Alignment
- **FR-014**: System shall map Russian standards to Chinese equivalents
- **FR-015**: System shall generate compliance certificates automatically
- **FR-016**: System shall maintain audit trails for regulatory inspections
